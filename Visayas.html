CEBU

It is the older city in the Philippines, the second largest metropolitant area and main gateway to other Visayas destination. Cebu popular for its cultural sites and their biggest festival Sinulog Festival.

BOHOL

Bohol is one of the most popular and tourist-friendly destination in the Philippines. This island province in the Central Visayas region attracts visitors from all over the world because of the most famous tourist spot. Chocolate Hills.

ILOILO

Ilolo hailed as the heart of the Philippines. It is the home of historical landmarks, beaches and islands, as well as heritage homes. The provinces pictureque natural wonders and the friendly locals will make this a destination that will make you feel like a second home.
