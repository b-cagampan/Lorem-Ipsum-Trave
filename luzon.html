INTRAMUROS

Intramuros is located at City of Manila, It is a historic and urban district. Intramuros means "within the walls" because it is encircled with a massive high stone and thich walls and moats.

I L O C O S

ILOCOS REGION

A famous province for agro-industrial businesses. They are know for historic sites, beaches and spanish colonial city of Vigan which is a UNESCO world heritage site found on Ilocos sur.

P A N G A S I N A N

PANGASINAN

It is a destination for tourist who wish to explore islands, beaches, and waterfalls. It is just 3 to 4 hours of drive from Manila. Pangasinan translate to "place of salt" as major producer of salt.
